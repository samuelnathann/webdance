Dance is a form of art that commonly occupies
movement of the body, frequently musical and to tune. It is executed as a
type of emotional appearance, communal relations, in a spiritual or performance background
in varies cultures. It is also sometimes used to express ideas or tell a story. Dance may also be observed
as a category of nonverbal interaction between humans or other animals, as in bee dances and
performance patterns such as a mating dance. Dance has its own substance, terminology,
skills, and methods, which has to be implicit and implemented to be dexterous in this art.
The elements of dance are the introductory notions and vocabulary for mounting
movement skills and also the understanding dance as an art type.
All these elements are concurrently present in a dance
or still in a short movement expression.
